Why Use ISKOVA?

Our reservation system is designed to make booking computer labs as simple and efficient as possible.

Easy Scheduling

Browse available dates and times with our intuitive calendar interface. See lab availability at a glance.

Multiple Labs

Choose from various computer labs across campus, each with different capacities and equipment.

Flexible Time Slots

Book labs for the exact duration you need, from one-hour sessions to full-day reservations.

Secure Booking

Get instant confirmation with QR code access passes that verify your reservation at lab entry.

How It Works

Follow these simple steps to reserve your computer lab session.

01

Select Date & Time

Choose your preferred date and available time slot from our booking calendar.

02

Choose a Lab

Browse available labs, see their capacity, and select the one that fits your needs.

03

Confirm Booking

Review your reservation details and confirm to receive your QR code access pass.

04

Access the Lab

Present your QR code at the lab entrance for quick and easy check-in.
